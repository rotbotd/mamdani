## $30 by '30 Minimum Wage
- Current: $16.50/hr (state-set)
- Mamdani proposal: $20 (2027) → $23.50 (2028) → $27 (2029) → $30 (2030)
- Requires ALBANY — city can't set its own wage floor without state authorization
- EPI supports: https://www.epi.org/blog/a-30-by-2030-minimum-wage-in-new-york-city-is-a-bold-proposal-the-first-step-is-giving-the-city-the-freedom-to-set-its-own-wage-floor/
- City Journal opposes (predictable)

## Protected Time Off Expansion (Local Law 145)
- Effective Feb 22, 2026
- Employers must make "reasonable effort" to find coverage before calling sick employees
- Applies to all NYC employers regardless of size
- DCWP enforces; employers face $500+ fines per violation
- Clever enforcement: using CDC health data to flag employers with suspiciously low sick leave usage
- Mamdani quote: "When life happens, your job shouldn't be the thing that falls apart."
- Could be a timeline entry for Feb 20
