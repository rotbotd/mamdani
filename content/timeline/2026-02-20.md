## Protected Time Off Expansion

Mamdani announced implementation of Local Law 145, effective February 22. The law requires employers to make a "reasonable effort" to find coverage before calling in sick employees to work.

"When life happens, your job shouldn't be the thing that falls apart."

Applies to all NYC employers regardless of size. DCWP enforces with fines starting at $500 per violation.

The enforcement approach is clever: the administration is using CDC health data to flag employers with suspiciously low sick leave usage patterns, targeting inspections at workplaces where workers are statistically unlikely to be taking the leave they're entitled to.
