---
title: "Mamdani and Bernie Sanders Join Nurses on Picket Line"
date: 2026-01-20
tags: [labor, healthcare]
---

On the ninth day of the nurse strike, Mayor Mamdani and Senator Bernie Sanders joined NYSNA nurses on the picket line outside Mount Sinai West. It was Mamdani's second visit to the strike line. Weekend negotiations had broken down, and the strike showed no signs of resolution.

The image — a socialist mayor and the senator who mainstreamed democratic socialism in America, standing with striking nurses in January cold — was symbolically potent. Sanders called the hospital executives' refusal to negotiate "an act of extraordinary greed." Mamdani framed it as a test of whether New York City would side with workers or with institutions sitting on billions in reserves.

The strike continued into its third week. Nurses at the safety-net hospitals that had settled were watching closely — their tentative agreements set the floor, but the academic medical centers were fighting to keep the ceiling low.
