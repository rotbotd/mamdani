---
title: "$50 Million for Parks in Underserved Neighborhoods"
date: 2026-03-05
tags: [infrastructure]
---

Mayor Mamdani announced a $50 million capital investment to reconstruct 10 parks in underserved neighborhoods. This is the kind of targeted investment that puts money where it's been historically absent—not the flashy Manhattan parks that already get attention, but the neighborhood parks in communities that have been neglected for decades.

Parks matter. They're public space that belongs to everyone, places where kids play and neighbors meet. The condition of a neighborhood's parks says a lot about how much the city values that neighborhood. This investment is a statement about priorities.
