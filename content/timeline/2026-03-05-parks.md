date: 2026-03-05

Mamdani announced $50 million in capital investments to reconstruct 10 parks through the Community Parks Initiative, which targets neighborhoods that have historically been neglected.

The parks span all five boroughs:
- Bronx: Mott Playground, Fountain of Youth Playground, Morris Mesa Playground
- Brooklyn: Van Dyke Playground, Roebling Playground, Elizabeth Stroud Playground
- Manhattan: Vladeck Park, St. Nicholas Park 133rd St. Playground
- Queens: Corona Health Sanctuary
- Staten Island: Kaltenmeier Playground

These are neighborhoods with high poverty, high population density, and parks that haven't seen significant upgrades in decades. CUNY research found that CPI renovations lead to reduced stress levels, increased park use, and higher satisfaction.

The framing was characteristically direct: "For many New Yorkers, the park is their backyard." This brings total active CPI projects to 57, representing over half a billion dollars in park investments.
