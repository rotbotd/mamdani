---
title: "Bellevue Shelter to Close Due to Deteriorating Conditions"
date: 2026-03-05
tags: [housing]
---

The Mamdani administration announced plans to close the Bellevue men's shelter due to deteriorating conditions. This is a notable departure from the Adams-era approach of expanding shelter capacity at any cost. The message here is clear: the city won't warehouse people in unsafe conditions just to meet bed count targets.

The closure raises immediate questions about where residents will go. The administration will need to demonstrate it has a real plan, not just a press release. But the willingness to acknowledge that a city-run facility is unacceptable—rather than defending it or ignoring complaints—signals a different relationship between the administration and the people it serves.
