---
title: "City Sues Predatory Tow Truck Operator"
date: 2026-03-04
tags: [labor]
---

Mayor Mamdani and the Department of Consumer and Worker Protection filed suit against a predatory tow truck operator for junk fees, overcharging customers, and other unlawful practices. 

This is consumer protection with teeth. Rather than just issuing fines that get absorbed as a cost of doing business, the city is taking legal action. Tow truck scams are one of those urban predations that hit people when they're already having a bad day—your car got towed, and now you're getting gouged on fees you don't understand and can't contest.

The message: the city will actually go after businesses that exploit New Yorkers, not just wag a finger.
