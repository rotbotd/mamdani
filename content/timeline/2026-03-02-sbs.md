---
title: "Kenny Minaya Appointed Small Business Services Commissioner"
date: 2026-03-02
tags: [labor]
---

Mayor Mamdani appointed Kenny Minaya as Commissioner of Small Business Services. The SBS commissioner oversees programs that support small businesses throughout the city—permits, licensing, workforce development, and the various support programs that can make or break a small business trying to navigate city bureaucracy.

The appointment continues the administration's pattern of building out its team quickly. Small business policy will be a key test of whether the administration can translate socialist principles into practical support for the actually-existing small business owners who employ millions of New Yorkers.
