---
title: "Administration Appointments"
---

A running list of Mayor Mamdani's hires and appointments, updated as of March 6, 2026.

## Top Leadership

**Dean Fuleihan** - First Deputy Mayor (announced Nov 10, 2025). At 74, Fuleihan served as first deputy mayor and budget director under de Blasio. His experience helps address concerns about Mamdani's youth and lack of management experience.

**Elle Bisgaard-Church** - Chief of Staff (announced Nov 10, 2025). Mamdani's former Assembly chief of staff and campaign manager. A DSA member, same age as the mayor.

**Anna Bahr** - Communications Director. Previously Bernie Sanders' communications director.

## Deputy Mayors

**Julia Kerson** - Deputy Mayor for Operations (announced Dec 31, 2025). Former deputy to Hochul's state director of operations, de Blasio administration veteran, worked on Second Avenue Subway extension.

**Leila Bozorg** - Deputy Mayor for Housing and Planning (announced Dec 19, 2025). Former executive director of housing under Adams, helped negotiate City of Yes, Obama-era HUD adviser.

**Julie Su** - Deputy Mayor for Labor and Worker Protection (announced Dec 19, 2025). Former acting U.S. Labor Secretary under Biden, never confirmed by Senate. Just moved to Brooklyn in March 2026.

**Helen Arteaga** - Deputy Mayor for Health and Human Services (announced Dec 30, 2025). Former CEO of NYC Health + Hospitals/Elmhurst, took that role during COVID.

## Budget and Legal

**Sherif Soliman** - Budget Director (announced Dec 18, 2025). Served under the past three mayors. Former commissioner of Department of Finance, experience with property tax reform.

**Ramzi Kassem** - Chief Counsel (announced Dec 30, 2025). Former Biden White House senior adviser on immigration, member of Mahmoud Khalil's legal team, CUNY law professor.

**Steven Banks** - Corporation Counsel (announced Dec 30, 2025, confirmed Feb 12, 2026). Former commissioner of Social Services under de Blasio, former attorney-in-chief at Legal Aid Society, defended right to shelter.

## Public Safety

**Jessica Tisch** - Police Commissioner (announced Nov 19, 2025). Retained from Adams administration. 17-year bureaucrat, three-time commissioner, from a billionaire family. Diverges from Mamdani on some policing issues.

**Lillian Bonsignore** - Fire Commissioner (announced Dec 23, 2025). First out gay and second female fire commissioner. Retired in 2022 as chief of EMS division.

**Stanley Richards** - Corrections Commissioner (announced Jan 31, 2026). First formerly incarcerated person to hold this position. Former Fortune Society executive.

## Education and Children

**Kamar Samuels** - Schools Chancellor (announced Dec 31, 2025). Former Manhattan District 3 superintendent, known for school mergers and improving literacy scores.

**Emmy Liss** - Director, Mayor's Office of Child Care. Shaped 3-K and pre-K under de Blasio. Central to universal child care implementation.

## Health and Social Services

**Erin Dalton** - Commissioner, Department of Social Services (appointed Feb 25, 2026). Former director of Allegheny County Department of Human Services in Pennsylvania.

**Dr. Alister Martin** - Health Commissioner (announced Jan 31, 2026). Emergency room physician from Massachusetts, Harvard Medical School, first Latino health commissioner.

**Dr. Mitchell Katz** - President/CEO, NYC Health + Hospitals (renominated Dec 29, 2025). Has held position since 2017.

**Lisa Scott-McKenzie** - Commissioner, Department for the Aging (announced Feb 10, 2026). 40-year veteran of NYC Health + Hospitals system.

## Housing and Planning

**Ahmed Tigani** - Buildings Commissioner (announced Dec 31, 2025). Former acting HPD commissioner under Adams, de Blasio alum, 15 years in housing.

**Dina Levy** - Commissioner, Housing Preservation and Development (announced Jan 4, 2026). From state's Homes and Community Renewal agency.

**Sideya Sherman** - Chair, City Planning Commission (announced Feb 24, 2026). Former commissioner of Office of Equity and Racial Justice under Adams.

**Cea Weaver** - Director, Mayor's Office to Protect Tenants (announced Jan 1, 2026). Former leader of Housing Justice for All, architect of 2019 tenant protection laws. Reviled by real estate industry.

## Transportation and Infrastructure

**Mike Flynn** - Transportation Commissioner (announced Jan 1, 2026). Former DOT employee, joined from private sector consulting.

**Lisa Garcia** - Commissioner, Department of Environmental Protection (announced Jan 27, 2026). Former EPA Region 2 administrator under Biden.

**Christina Farrell** - Commissioner, Emergency Management (announced Feb 16, 2026). 23-year veteran of the agency, led through Sandy and COVID.

## Labor and Consumer Protection

**Sam Levine** - Commissioner, Department of Consumer and Worker Protection (announced Dec 22, 2025). Former head of Bureau of Consumer Protection at FTC under Biden.

**Renee Campion** - Commissioner, Office of Labor Relations (reappointed Jan 27, 2026). Retained from Adams administration.

## Other Commissioners

**Tricia Shimamura** - Parks Commissioner (announced Jan 17, 2026). Former Manhattan borough parks commissioner, ran for Council in 2021.

**Yume Kitasei** - Commissioner, Department of Citywide Administrative Services (announced Jan 27, 2026). Former chief of staff to First Deputy Mayor Sheena Wright. Also a speculative fiction novelist.

**Nadia Shihata** - Commissioner, Department of Investigation (nominated Feb 12, 2026). Former federal prosecutor, led R. Kelly prosecution. Pending Council approval.

**Diya Vij** - Commissioner, Department of Cultural Affairs (announced Feb 28, 2026). First South Asian to hold position. Former VP at Powerhouse Arts.

**Kenny Minaya** - Commissioner, Department of Small Business Services (announced Mar 2, 2026). Former deputy commissioner at DCWP, started as housing attorney defending immigrant tenants.

**Sharun Goodwin** - Commissioner, Department of Probation (announced Jan 27, 2026). Career DOB employee from 1986 intern to deputy commissioner, came out of retirement.

**Sandra Escamilla-Davies** - Commissioner, Department of Youth and Community Development (announced Jan 31, 2026). Former positions at Youth Development Institute and Children's Aid.

**Yesenia Mata** - Commissioner, Department of Veterans' Services (announced Jan 31, 2026). First Latina in position, Army military police sergeant.

**Vilda Vera Mayuga** - Commissioner, OATH (announced Jan 31, 2026). Retained from Adams administration where she led DCWP.

**Midori Valdivia** - Chair, Taxi and Limousine Commission (nominated Jan 13, 2026). MTA board member, former TLC deputy commissioner. Pending Council approval.

## Special Offices and Advisers

**Jahmila Edwards** - Director, Intergovernmental Affairs (announced Dec 17, 2025). Former DC 37 associate director for 11 years, de Blasio alum.

**Tascha Van Auken** - Director, Office of Mass Engagement (announced Jan 2, 2026). Led campaign's mass volunteer mobilization.

**Stephanie Silkowski** - Director, Office of Appointments (announced Jan 27, 2026). Former director at The Bridge Project.

**Louise Yeung** - Chief Climate Officer (announced Dec 31, 2025). Former climate chief for Comptroller Lander, de Blasio DOT alum.

**Lisa Gelobter** - Chief Technology Officer (announced Feb 10, 2026). Part-inventor of GIFs, former chief digital service officer at U.S. Dept of Education under Obama.

**Nisha Agarwal** - Commissioner, Mayor's Office for People with Disabilities (announced Feb 10, 2026). Led Mayor's Office of Immigrant Affairs under de Blasio.

**Faiza Ali** - Commissioner, Mayor's Office of Immigrant Affairs (announced Feb 17, 2026). Former deputy chief of staff to Council Speaker Adrienne Adams.

**Ana María Archila** - Commissioner, International Affairs (announced Feb 17, 2026). Former WFP co-director, ran for lieutenant governor, co-founded Make the Road NY.

**Rafael Espinal** - Director, Mayor's Office of Media and Entertainment (announced Jan 12, 2026). Former City Council member, former Freelancers Union president.

**Afua Atta-Mensah** - Director, Office of Equity and Racial Justice (announced Jan 15, 2026). Campaign political director, former ED of Community Voices Heard.

**Annabel Clarke** - Chair, Commission on Human Rights (announced Jan 7, 2026). From Legal Services NYC.

**Phylisa Wisdom** - Director, Office to Combat Antisemitism (announced Feb 4, 2026). Executive director of New York Jewish Agenda.

**Maya Handa** - World Cup Czar (announced Jan 27, 2026). General election campaign manager.

**Ali Najmi** - Chair, Mayor's Advisory Committee on the Judiciary (announced Jan 2, 2026). Mamdani's election attorney and longtime friend.
