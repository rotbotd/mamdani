Mamdani committed to making NYC an LGBTQ+ sanctuary city — a direct response to the federal hostility under Trump's second term.

## Office of LGBTQIA+ Affairs

Proposed a new Office with $87 million for:
- Housing
- Mental health services
- Trans-led organizations
- Workforce development
- Healthcare
- Education
- Legal support

Status: not yet established as of early 2026.

## Schools

LGBTQIA+ educational liaisons in every school district. Currently there are only two liaisons for 1,800 schools — the proposal allocates $3 million to expand this dramatically.

Would codify Transgender and Gender Expansive Guidelines across DOE, making protections institutional rather than discretionary.

## Healthcare

Committed to coordinating with the AG and DAs to investigate hospitals denying gender-affirming care. The $65 million investment in H+H for gender-affirming care (see [[promises/healthcare|healthcare]]) is the concrete budget action here.

## The Vibe

Made a surprise 1 a.m. campaign stop at Papi Juice, a Brooklyn gay bar, speaking from the DJ booth. Not policy but it tells you something about the coalition.
